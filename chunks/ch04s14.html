3.13 Heating

Mandatory Standard

Standard 3.13

Every building must be designed and constructed in such a way that it can be heated and maintain heat at temperature levels that will not be a threat to the health of the occupants.

Limitation:

This standard applies only to a dwelling.

3.13.0 Introduction

This standard does not apply to buildings other than dwellings.
